  • How does Rapid Finance make money??

    Rapid Finance is run by a licensed SMC broker. Which means we are paid a commission for each insurance policy we sell, by the insurance companies. Does that mean you pay anything extra, other than your insurance policy premium? Nope. The commission is paid for by the insurer and not by you, the customer..

  • Does the commission make us biased for or against any insurance company?

    The answer is simply no, we cannot be biased as a broker. A broker is a customer's representative and is always for the customer and not for the insurance company.

  • Is it cheaper to buy insurance through Rapid Finance?

    Financial Product is a regulated market. No Finance company, neither Rapid Finance nor any other agent can give you a deal cheaper than what the insurance company has set. It is illegal to do so. The best way to save money on insurance premiums is by comparing various insurance plans available and making an informed choice.
